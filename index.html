Common Dream Symbols

Flying

Often represents freedom, ambition, or a desire to escape from life's pressures.

Being Chased

Suggests you're avoiding an issue or fear in your waking life that needs attention.

Losing Teeth

May indicate anxiety about appearance, communication, or feelings of powerlessness.

Houses/Rooms

Different rooms often represent aspects of yourself or your life that need exploration.

Water

Represents emotions - calm water suggests peace while turbulent water indicates emotional turmoil.

Vehicles

Symbolize your life's direction - who's driving and the vehicle's condition offer clues.
